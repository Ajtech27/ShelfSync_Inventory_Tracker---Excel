## ShelfSync_Inventory_Tracker-Excel
**Domain:** Retail Operations

A fully automated inventory management system for a fictional retail business tracking 36 SKUs across 8 product categories. Auto-flags low stock and out-of-stock items in real time as quantities are updated.

**4 Sheets:** Dashboard · Inventory · Stock Movement Log · Category Summary

**Key features:**
- Live KPI cards — Total SKUs, Stock Value, Low Stock count, Out of Stock count
- Automatic alert table that updates from the Inventory sheet
- Stock movement log with running balance for full audit trail
- COUNTIF, SUMIF, and nested IF formulas for automated alerts
- Conditional formatting — red, amber, and green rows based on stock levels
- Blue editable inputs and black calculated formulas throughout

**Tools:** Excel · COUNTIF/SUMIF · Nested IF · Conditional Formatting · Cross-sheet References
